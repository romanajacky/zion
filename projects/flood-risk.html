Flood Risk Mapping

Coastal Region, Kenya | 2023

Project Overview

This project developed high-resolution flood hazard maps for Kenya's coastal region, identifying vulnerable zones, evacuation routes, and critical infrastructure at risk from both riverine and coastal flooding.

Key Outcomes

  • Mapped 250km² of flood-prone areas
  • Identified 15 critical infrastructure facilities at risk
  • Developed 8 optimal evacuation routes
  • Protected 50,000+ residents with early warning integration

Methodology

Data Sources

Sentinel-1 SAR imagery, TanDEM-X elevation data, rainfall records, and historical flood data

Modeling Approach

Hydrological modeling using HEC-RAS combined with machine learning for flood susceptibility mapping

Flood Hazard Zones

  • High Risk (100-year flood zone): 45 km² - Immediate action required
  • Medium Risk (200-year flood zone): 85 km² - Monitoring recommended
  • Low Risk: 120 km² - Standard preparedness
