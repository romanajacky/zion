Risk Assessment & Analysis

Identify and evaluate natural disaster risks using advanced geospatial analysis

Service Overview

Our Risk Assessment service helps communities, governments, and organizations identify, evaluate, and prepare for natural hazards including floods, landslides, wildfires, and droughts. Using satellite data and predictive modeling, we deliver actionable risk maps and vulnerability assessments.

Risk Types We Analyze

  • High Flood risk mapping and inundation modeling
  • High Wildfire susceptibility and spread prediction
  • Medium Landslide hazard zonation
  • Medium Drought monitoring and prediction
  • Low Coastal erosion and sea-level rise impact

What We Deliver

  • High-resolution hazard zonation maps
  • Vulnerability and exposure assessments
  • Evacuation route planning
  • Critical infrastructure risk analysis
  • Comprehensive risk assessment reports

Methodology

Flood Risk Assessment

We use hydrological modeling combined with DEM analysis, rainfall data, and historical flood records to create detailed flood hazard maps.

Wildfire Risk Assessment

Our analysis incorporates vegetation indices (NDVI), topography, weather patterns, and historical fire data to predict fire-prone areas.

Landslide Hazard Zonation

We analyze slope, aspect, geology, soil type, and rainfall thresholds to identify landslide susceptible zones.

Technologies Used
