Custom Satellite Products

Pre-processed satellite imagery and custom GIS data layers designed for your specific requirements

Service Overview

We provide ready-to-use satellite imagery products tailored to your project needs. From vegetation indices to thermal maps, our custom products save you time and ensure data consistency for your analysis workflows.

Standard Products

🌿 NDVI & Vegetation Health

Normalized Difference Vegetation Index maps for crop health monitoring, forest density assessment, and vegetation stress detection.

10m resolution 5-day revisit

🌡️ Thermal & Moisture Maps

Land Surface Temperature (LST) and Soil Moisture content maps for drought monitoring, urban heat island analysis, and irrigation planning.

30m resolution Thermal band

🗺️ Custom Data Layers

Any GIS-ready data layer you need: land cover classification, digital elevation models, slope analysis, aspect maps, and more.

Fully customizable Any format

Satellite Data Sources

  • Landsat 8 & 9 - 30m resolution, 16-day revisit
  • Sentinel-2 - 10m resolution, 5-day revisit
  • Sentinel-1 - SAR data, 6-day revisit
  • MODIS - 250m resolution, daily revisit
  • Planet Scope - 3m resolution, daily revisit (commercial)
  • Maxar (DigitalGlobe) - 0.5m resolution, on-demand

Custom Processing Options

  • Atmospheric correction
  • Cloud masking and gap filling
  • Pan-sharpening (improve resolution)
  • Mosaicking and tile assembly
  • Reprojection to any coordinate system
  • Time-series composites (monthly, seasonal, annual)

Delivery Formats

We deliver products in your preferred format:

  • GeoTIFF, Cloud Optimized GeoTIFF (COG)
  • Shapefile, GeoJSON, KML
  • Google Earth Engine asset
  • ArcGIS online service
  • NetCDF, HDF
